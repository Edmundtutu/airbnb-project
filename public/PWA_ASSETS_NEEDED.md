## Icon Design Guidelines:

1. **Use square icons** - All icons should be square (1:1 aspect ratio)
2. **Safe zone** - Keep important content within 80% of the icon (for maskable icons)
3. **Format** - Use PNG for all icons except the masked-icon which should be SVG
4. **Background** - Icons should have a background color (transparent backgrounds may not display well)
5. **Brand colors** - Use your brand colors (#00ac90 for primary)
